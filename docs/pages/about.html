About¶

Authors¶

This repository was developed at the University of Tennessee in Knoxville, TN through a collaboration between research groups led by Dr. Konstantinos Vogiatzis, assistant professor of theoretical and computational chemistry, and Dr. Vasileios Maroulas, professor of mathematics. The code was developed by Jacob Townsend. Other authors included Cassie Putman Mucci and John H. Hymel.
